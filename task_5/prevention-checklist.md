# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
- Always verify unknown links before clicking
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
- Keep your apps and devices updated for security patches
- Be cautious of calls/messages creating urgency or fear

##  UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown “collect” requests on UPI apps
- Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN
- Double-check the receiver’s details before sending money
- Use only official apps from trusted sources (Play Store/App Store)

##  Reporting Cybercrime
- Report online at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930

Taking these precautions can help protect you from cyber fraud and ensure a safer digital experience.
